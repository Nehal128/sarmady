WHO WE ARE

Sarmady is a Full-Fledged Digital Media House that was established in 2001 , with the mission of making “infinite digital
innovations”. Today Sarmady is aiming at becoming the number one Digital Destination in the region. MENA's #1DD.

Sarmady goes beyond crafting and publishing premium portals like FilGoal.com and Reyada.com in Sports, ContactCars.com in Automotive, Akhbarak.net and DarAlAkhbar.com in News, FilFan.com in Entertainment and Cinema Guide mobile application, among many other services with dominant traffic presence on Mobile and Apps for years now.

Sarmady's own experience in designing and developing great Content Management Systems (CMSs) has given it a unique position in the market making other renown publishers like Youm7.com , ElWatanNews.com, AlMasryAlYoum.com in Egypt, Al-Sharq.com in Qatar and Alittihad.ae in the UAE, design and develop their websites with us at some point in their history. The design and development jobs would include Desktop, Mobile, Responsive sites, Android and iOS Apps.


One of Sarmady's greatest assets of business is Ethics and Reputation. Great reputation has always helped us win the hearts of our users and clients, and we will always continue to deliver along this line, aiming and not only winning their hearts, but even delight them.

Being the owners of top-ranked premium portals from both quality and traffic perspectives, we also distribute our content through various channels aside from our domains and SMS alerts, we provide content creation services for third parties on branded and white-labeled basis. Entities like Microsoft, KSA's STC, Fatafeat and the Egyptian Government are just few examples of clients that have trusted our capabilities of partnership in doing great business.

On top of that, we provide digital advertising services for our clients on our properties, via Operators' SMS, on Google Display Network (GDN), Social Media networks like Facebook, Instagram, Twitter and LinkedIn. Our advertising services go beyond serving banners; we tailor custom made digital solutions, short and long term campaigns, premium quality SMS services.

Despite being partially acquired by Vodafone Egypt in 2008 and fully acquired later in 2011, Sarmady remains to run autonomously and has direct commercial agreements with the other Egyptian Telecom operators like Orange and Etisalat.

We proudly believe that the majority of businesses in Egypt and the region should be on our honorable client list, if you are not one already, please give us this pleasure by contacting us to explore potential cooperation areas.
